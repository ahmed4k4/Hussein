    Glass Sycori
    We specialize in high-quality Sycorite glass works ranging from 5 mm to 20 mm thickness.
    Sycorite glass is processed by heating it to a temperature of 680 degrees Celsius and then rapidly cooling it, 
    giving it exceptional strength to withstand impacts, high temperatures, and other factors. Unlike regular glass, 
    Sycorite glass shatters into small, harmless pieces upon impact, making it safer for use in various applications.
    
    Uses of Sycorite Glass:
    - Widely used in storefronts and large commercial centers.
    - Ideal for shower cabins.
    - Used in interior partitions for offices, companies, and modern residential units.
    - Suitable for balconies, garden fences, and swimming pool enclosures.
    - Utilized in glass doors and windows for hotels, companies, malls, and storefronts.
    - Sycorite glass is also commonly used in vehicles like cars, trains, and airplanes, as well as in commercial storefronts.
    
    Sycorite glass is one of the safest types of glass, as it breaks into small, harmless pieces upon impact, unlike regular glass.
